Khao man gai is Thailand’s most beloved street food!

What is khao man gai?

Thai chicken-and-rice. Organic free-range chicken slowly poached with garlic and ginger; Served over Thai jasmine rice cooked with the poaching liquid, garlic, ginger, and Thai herbs; Accompanied by a garlicky ginger sauce that balances the four main Thai tastes of salty, sweet, sour, and spicy, that ties the whole dish together; Garnished with cilantro and cucumber; And served with a light chicken soup for sipping between bites. One bite of chicken, rice and sauce and you’ll understand why Thai people love khao man gai.

We heart KMG! And we want everyone to know how delicious khao man gai is. The wife and husband team behind Khao Man Gai NY, Emorn (a Thai native) and her husband Eric, have shared khao man gai together at countless Thai street stalls and food markets all over Thailand. It is their favorite Thai food. When they decided to start a food business, they both agreed it had to be khao man gai, an authentic Thai street food dish known all over Thailand, but not typically offered in Thai restaurants. Just like in Thailand, Khao Man Gai NY focuses its efforts on preparing this single dish and utilizes only the freshest high quality ingredients. Khao Man Gai NY is excited to share its authentic Thai street food with all of New York. Thank you to our fans, family, and friends for all your support!
